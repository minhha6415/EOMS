Currently, order management is handled manually using Excel and messaging platforms such as Facebook and Zalo.

This leads to several issues:
- Orders may be duplicated, missed, or processed incorrectly (e.g., payment failures still recorded as successful orders, out-of-stock items still being ordered)
- Inventory is not updated in real-time, causing inconsistencies
- Staff must manually verify orders, increasing workload and human error
- Lack of visibility into order status, especially for failed or returned orders

As a result, the business faces operational inefficiencies, increased error rates, and poor customer experience.
